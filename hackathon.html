Frequently Asked Questions

Can I work individually?

Yes! You can participate as an individual or form a group. Either way, at least one registered academy participant must be involved.

Can I include people outside the program?

Absolutely. External collaborators are welcome. Just ensure all team members are listed in your submission with complete details.

What if I don't have coding experience?

This is a learning opportunity. Use available Meta AI tools and seek mentorship. You can also collaborate with others who have technical skills.

How are social media metrics evaluated?

All metrics are normalized by team size. If you have 4 team members and 400 views, it counts as 100 per person. This ensures fairness.

What happens if I don't submit?

Submission is mandatory for program completion and certificate issuance. Without a valid submission, you cannot receive the professional certificate.

Can I submit in a language other than English?

Project documentation should be in English for fair evaluation by the judging panel. However, user feedback can be in any language with translations provided.

What if my AI agent isn't perfect?

Perfection isn't required‚Äîlearning and genuine effort are what matters. The judges value innovation, problem-solving approach, and real-world testing over polish.

When will winners be announced?

Winners will be announced on Saturday, December 20, 2025 during the final review session. Top three winners will be contacted immediately.
