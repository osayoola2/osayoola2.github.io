Program Overview

Welcome to the Meta AI Developer Academy!

A 7-week intensive training program in Agentic AI Systems development using Meta's cutting-edge AI tools and platforms. This program is fully funded by Meta.

Course Title: Development of Agentic AI Systems Using Meta AI Resources

Duration: 7 Weeks (November 1 - December 19, 2025)

Format: Live online sessions + Hands-on projects + Hackathons

Funding: 100% Sponsored by Meta

Implementation Partner: RAIN Nigeria

Program Lead: Dr. Olusola Ayoola

About the Funding: Meta is investing in African talent development by providing this world-class AI training completely free of charge. This includes access to Meta's proprietary AI tools, expert instruction, certification, and competition prizes.

What You'll Learn

This intensive program will transform you into an expert in Agentic AI Systems. You'll gain hands-on experience with:

Meta's Llama Models

PyTorch
